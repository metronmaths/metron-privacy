Metron Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

No Personal Data Collected

Metron does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The app has no user accounts, no registration, and no analytics or advertising.

Local Storage Only

Your preferences (theme, angle mode, display precision, number format, and startup page) and calculation history are stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device.

AI Assistant (Pro+AI tier)

If you subscribe to the Pro+AI plan, the AI Maths Assistant sends your messages to Google's Gemini API to generate responses. Only the text of your conversation is transmitted — no personal information is included. This data is subject to Google's privacy policy.

The AI Assistant is only active when you use it. Free and Pro users make no external network requests.

In-App Purchases

Pro and Pro+AI subscriptions are processed through Google Play. Payment information is handled entirely by Google and is never seen or stored by Metron.

No Third-Party Analytics or Advertising

Metron does not integrate any analytics, advertising, or crash reporting services.

Children's Privacy

Because we do not collect any personal data, Metron is safe to use by people of all ages.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact: metronmaths@gmail.com
