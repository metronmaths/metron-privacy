No Data Collection

Metron does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The app has no user accounts, no registration, and no analytics or advertising.

Local Storage Only

Your preferences (theme, angle mode, display precision, number format, and startup page) are stored locally on your device using browser localStorage. This data never leaves your device.

No Internet Access Required

Metron functions entirely offline. No network requests are made during normal use.

No Third-Party Services

Metron does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or crash reporting services.

Children's Privacy

Because we do not collect any data, Metron is safe to use by people of all ages.
